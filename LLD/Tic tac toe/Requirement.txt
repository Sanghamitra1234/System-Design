Tic Tac Toe Game
1) There will the 2 players
2) The board will be of size 3x3
3) There will be 2 symbols
4) A scoreboard can be maintained.
3) The players will take turns to make their move. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
4) When all of the squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 of their marks in a line, then the game ends in a draw.
